How to use

  1. Turn on Bluetooth on your phone and TV (make TV discoverable in Settings → Bluetooth).
  2. Tap “FIND MY TV” then choose your TV from the Chrome device picker.
  3. Tap “HEAR ON PHONE” to start demo audio (BLE packets if available, otherwise microphone or test tone).
  4. Adjust volume, mute, and keep the page open; screen lock can still keep audio running.

Note: Browsers currently support only Bluetooth Low Energy GATT, not full A2DP audio sink, so real TV audio over Bluetooth needs native support or custom hardware. This app demonstrates the full pipeline and uses realistic fallbacks.

You are not on HTTPS. For full Bluetooth support, run on HTTPS or localhost:8000.

Web Bluetooth is only available on Chromium-based browsers like Chrome or Edge on supported platforms.
